This is a moderate-intensity workout split that I follow every week that targets different muscle groups in my body to gain strength and definition. You can perform variants of the workouts or substitue them for others to match your liking.

Regular physical activity is one of the most important things you can do for your health. Being physically active can improve your brain health, help manage weight, reduce the risk of disease, strengthen bones and muscles, and improve your ability to do everyday activities.

Being physically active can improve your brain health, help manage weight, reduce the risk of disease, strengthen bones and muscles, and improve your ability to do everyday activities. Adults who sit less and do any amount of moderate-to-vigorous physical activity gain some health benefits.

Workout Routine

  1. Sunday - Rest
    • Rest because it's the Lord's Day and the gym is closed
  2. Monday - Legs
    • Glute-Focused Hyperextension (4x15)
    • Hip Thrusts w/ 10 sec hold (4x8)
    • Seated Calf Machine (4x12)
    • Leg Press (4x12)
    • Romanian Deadlifts (4x10)
    • Bulgarian Split Squats (4x8)
    • Cable Kickbacks (4x10)
    • Cable Side Raises (4x10)
  3. Tuesday - Upper Body
    • Weighted Shoulder Shrugs (3x15)
    • Seated Shoulder Press (4x10)
    • Reverse Tricep Press (4x10)
    • Seated Lat Pulldowns (4x10)
    • Narrow Grip Pulldowns (4x10)
    • Narrow Grip Row (4x10)
    • Wide Grip Row (4x10)
    • Cable Pulldown (4x10)
    • Cable Delt Flies (4x10)
  4. Wednesday - Rest
    • Rest because it's the middle of the week
  5. Thursday - Legs
    • Glute-Focused Hyperextension (4x15)
    • Hip Thrusts w/ 10 sec hold (4x8)
    • Seated Calf Machine (4x12)
    • Leg Press (4x12)
    • Romanian Deadlifts (4x10)
    • Bulgarian Split Squats (4x8)
    • Cable Kickbacks (4x10)
    • Cable Side Raises (4x10)
  6. Friday - Cardio/Core
    • 30-45 Minutes on Treadmill (12 incline at 3.5 mph)
    • Knee to Chest Crunches(4x15)
    • Clap Crunches (4x10)
    • Rope Crunches (3x15)
    • Plank Twists (4x15)
    • Straight-Arm Plank (2x 30 seconds)
    • Forearm Plank (2x 30 seconds)
  7. (Sometimes) Saturday - Legs
    • Glute-Focused Hyperextension (4x15)
    • Hip Thrusts w/ 10 sec hold (4x8)
    • Seated Calf Machine (4x12)
    • Leg Press (4x12)
    • Romanian Deadlifts (4x10)
    • Bulgarian Split Squats (4x8)
    • Cable Kickbacks (4x10)
    • Cable Side Raises (4x10)
